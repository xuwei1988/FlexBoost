1. Overview

Flexchar (“the App”) is a companion application that connects to FlexChar / ESP32-S3 charging devices over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). It is used for device discovery and connection, live status, history, parameter configuration, and firmware updates.

The App does not require user registration or login. We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Information you provide or initiate

  • Firmware files (for example .bin or .txt) that you choose from your device for updates.
  • History or log exports you share through the system share sheet (you choose the destination app).

2.2 Information from connected devices (via BLE)

  • Bluetooth device identifiers, advertised names, and signal strength.
  • Device serial numbers, firmware versions, operating status, and charging / CAN-related telemetry.
  • Charging history, session records, and decoded signal data synced from the device.

2.3 Information stored locally on your iPhone

  • Recently connected device information for offline display.
  • History records, CAN frames, UI presets, and related app data in local storage.

This data stays on your device unless you explicitly share or export it. We do not automatically upload charging or history data to our business servers.

2.4 Network communication

The App connects to https://flex-boost.vercel.app to check firmware versions and download update packages. Requests may include app/version metadata and firmware manifest or file metadata (version numbers, file names, checksums). We do not transmit your name, phone number, Apple ID, or precise location through this service.

2.5 Information we do not collect

  • Precise location (the App does not request location permission).
  • Contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera (unless stated in a future update).
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data.
  • Account credentials (no sign-in is required).

3. How We Use Information

  • Discover, connect to, and communicate with your charging equipment.
  • Display live status, history, and diagnostic information.
  • Store records locally so you can review them offline.
  • Check for and download firmware updates.
  • Export or share data when you choose to do so.

4. Storage and Retention

App data is stored on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. We do not operate a cloud account database tied to your charging records in the current version of the App.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple iOS / TestFlight — app distribution and system permission prompts; governed by Apple’s policies.
  • FlexBoost firmware service (flex-boost.vercel.app, hosted on Vercel) — firmware manifests and file downloads. Vercel Blob stores firmware files and version metadata. Standard hosting access logs may be retained by the platform.
  • Open-source components (Bluetooth, file picking, sharing libraries) run on your device as part of the App.

The FlexBoost management dashboard on this site is intended for internal firmware administration and is not offered as a public consumer service.

6. Permissions (aligned with iOS prompts)

  • Bluetooth — scan for, connect to, and sync data with your device; background Bluetooth may be used during firmware updates.
  • Photos / files — only when you choose a firmware or related file from your library.

7. Your Choices

  • Manage Bluetooth and related permissions in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Remove local App data by uninstalling the App.
  • Contact us at support@vertech-pro.com for privacy questions.

8. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date on this page. Material changes may also be communicated in the App when appropriate.

1. 概述

Flexchar（「本 App」）是一款通过低功耗蓝牙（BLE）连接 FlexChar / ESP32-S3 充电设备的配套应用，用于设备发现与连接、实时状态查看、历史记录、参数配置及固件升级。

本 App 不要求用户注册或登录。我们不会出售您的个人信息。

2. 我们处理的信息

2.1 您主动提供或发起的操作

  • 您从本机选择的固件文件（如 .bin、.txt），用于设备升级。
  • 您通过系统分享功能导出的历史或日志（分享目标由您选择）。

2.2 通过蓝牙从设备获得的信息

  • 蓝牙设备标识符、广播名称、信号强度。
  • 设备序列号、固件版本、运行状态及充电 / CAN 相关 telemetry。
  • 从设备同步的充电历史、会话记录及解码后的信号数据。

2.3 存储在您 iPhone 本地的信息

  • 最近连接的设备信息（便于离线显示）。
  • 历史记录、CAN 帧、UI 预设等本地数据库中的 App 数据。

上述数据保存在您的设备上，除非您主动分享或导出。我们不会自动将充电或历史数据上传至业务服务器。

2.4 网络通信

本 App 会连接 https://flex-boost.vercel.app 以检查固件版本并下载更新包。请求可能包含 App/版本元数据及固件 manifest 或文件元数据（版本号、文件名、校验值等）。我们不会通过该服务传输您的姓名、电话、Apple ID 或精确地理位置。

2.5 我们不会收集的信息

  • 精确地理位置（本 App 不请求定位权限）。
  • 通讯录、日历、麦克风、相机（除非未来版本另行说明）。
  • 广告标识符或跨 App 追踪数据。
  • 账号凭证（无需登录）。

3. 我们如何使用信息

  • 发现、连接并与您的充电设备通信。
  • 显示实时状态、历史与诊断信息。
  • 在本地保存记录，供您离线查看。
  • 检查并下载固件更新。
  • 在您主动操作时导出或分享数据。

4. 存储与保留

App 数据保存在您的设备上，直至您卸载 App 或清除其数据。当前版本 App 不运营与用户充电记录绑定的云端账户数据库。

5. 第三方服务

  • Apple iOS / TestFlight — 应用分发与系统权限管理，受 Apple 政策约束。
  • FlexBoost 固件服务（flex-boost.vercel.app，托管于 Vercel）— 固件 manifest 与文件下载。Vercel Blob 存储固件文件及版本元数据。托管平台可能保留标准访问日志。
  • 开源组件（蓝牙、文件选择、分享库）作为 App 的一部分在您的设备上运行。

本站的 FlexBoost 管理面板主要用于内部固件管理，不对公众提供消费级服务。

6. 权限说明（与 iOS 系统弹窗一致）

  • 蓝牙 — 扫描、连接设备并同步数据；固件升级期间可能使用后台蓝牙。
  • 相册 / 文件 — 仅在您主动选择固件或相关文件时使用。

7. 您的选择

  • 可在 iOS「设置 → 隐私与安全性」中管理蓝牙等权限。
  • 卸载 App 可删除本地数据。
  • 如有隐私相关问题，请联系 support@vertech-pro.com。

8. 儿童隐私

本 App 不面向 13 周岁以下儿童，也不会故意收集该年龄段儿童的个人信息。
